Game: Pacman
_________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Pacman : ' > ', ' < ', ' v ', ' ^ '. 

	Pacman is the main character of the game.
	Pacman moves around the maze and 
	tries to eat all of the candies ( points (.), power pellets (*) ).
_________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Ghosts : 'M'
	
	Ghosts are enemies of pacman. Ghosts move around the maze and 
	try to eat pacman. If pacman has asctivated super power, ghosts
	can be killed. 
_________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Points/Dots : '.' 
		
	Pacman tries to eat all of these. If it eats points and boosts, 
	he wins.
_________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Power pellets : '*'
	
	Pacman tries to eat all of these. If it eats points and boosts, 
	he wins.
_________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Fruits : 's'
	
	Pacman tries to eat all of these. Fruits are bonus, and appear once
	in a while
_________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

HOW TO CONTROL THE GAME:
	
	pacman controls and menu controls

	'w' - move up 
	's' - move down
	'd' - move right
	'a' - move left
	ESC - open Menu	
	ENTER - current option
